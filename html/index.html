"I have no special talents. I am passionately curious." - Einstein

I like this quote from Albert Einstein. In my opinon, it fits me well and also describes the attitude of Swiss people and a part of the culture of Switzerland. I believe, we are extremly curious and like to explore ideas and experiment new things. Even in a mountainous and quiet unexciting country, people usually feel welcome and appreciate their time their!

The following sections give an overview of the culture into which I grown up. It is a patchwork of my memories. It tells the stories about the inventions I would be fascinated with, about the artists I would study and read about, about the places I would go to and the dishes I would cook. I hope you will enjoy reading about it! Who knows, you may discover interesting mysteries!

About

Switzerland has many facets and many personalities. We are valued for our honesty, simplicity and sense of discretion. We like to create and laugh about ourself even if we are usually quite serious. Every country has an history, every family has an history. I tried to give you an overview of my culture and the environment and things I discovered when I grew up! Time to flight to this country and hope you will enjoy it!
